<h4>SGS Welcomes Sekolah Tinggi Perempuan Raja Isteri (STPRI), Brunei</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/stpri_brunei.jpg">
</div>
<p>It was a week filled with excitement as SGS hosted students and teachers
from Sekolah Tinggi Perempuan Raja Isteri (STPRI), Brunei, for the Inaugural
School Twinning Programme in May. Over five enriching days, our Brunei
friends journeyed with SGS buddies to experience school life in Singapore,
including hands-on Applied Learning Modules in Interior Design, Forensic
Science, Early Childhood Education, and Business &amp; Entrepreneurship.
The week was filled with wonderful cultural exchanges, from a traditional
kompang welcome and a gulingtangan performance by STPRI students, to a
communal Nasi Ambeng meal and Teh Tarik-making session at Kampong Glam,
whilst exploring iconic Singapore landmarks. We are grateful for the friendships
forged and memories made and we look forward to many more years of meaningful
ties between SGS and STPRI!</p>
<h4>SGS celebrated International Friendship Day 2026 with International French School, Singapore</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/IFD.jpg">
</div>
<p>SGS celebrated International Friendship Day with a vibrant celebration
of global connections! Students explored ASEAN cultures through interactive
booths and CCE ambassadors brought global flavours to life sharing diverse
perspectives through international cuisines. We were delighted that our
friends from the International French School joined in the festivities,
enriching our day with cultural games and food, and impromptu busking performances
of French pop songs.</p>
<h4>SGS Welcomes His Excellency Peter Storer, Ambassador of Austria</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Austria_Ambassador.jpg">
</div>
<p>SGS had the pleasure of hosting His Excellency Peter Storer, Ambassador
of Austria to Singapore, for an engaging and insightful talk on international
diplomacy.</p>
<p>The session brought Social Studies concepts to life, as students deepened
their understanding of diplomacy and international relations. Through the
talk, interactive quiz, and Q&amp;A session, they explored how nations
built bridges, and how countries work together - in wide-ranging areas
including trade, culture, and global initiatives - to bring about better
lives for their people. Students were better able to appreciate the importance
of respect, cooperation, and communication in shaping a more connected
and harmonious world.</p>

<h4>SGS Welcomes Choi Hung Estate Catholic Secondary School</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Choi_Hung_Estate_Catholic_Secondary_School.jpg">
</div>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Choi_Hung_Estate_Catholic_Secondary_School__1_.jpg">
</div>
<p>SGS had the pleasure of hosting students and teachers from Choi Hung Estate
Catholic Secondary School (Hong Kong SAR, China) as part of International
Friendship Day (IFD) celebrations on 11 April 2025.</p>
<p>Our visitors participated in a special IFD concert and presented on their
school, Co-Curricular Activities (CCA), and academic experiences. Together
with their buddies, they attended lessons and enjoyed a hands-on experience
when they proudly prepared and savoured Pad Thai during a Food and Consumer
Education (FCE) lesson.</p>
<p>This meaningful exchange provided an excellent platform for our students
to develop communication skills and global literacy, as they interacted
with peers from Hong Kong, fostering cross-cultural friendships and understanding.</p>
