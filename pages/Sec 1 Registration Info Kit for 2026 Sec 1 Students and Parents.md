<h4><strong><u>Section I - Eligibility for Higher Mother Tongue Languages (HMTL)</u></strong></h4>
<p>With effect from 2026, students who meet either of the following criteria
will be eligible to apply for HMTL:</p>
<p>a. Achievement of AL1 or AL2 in their PSLE Mother Tongue Language; or</p>
<p>b. Attainment of Distinction or Merit in their HMTL examination.</p>
<p>Parents/Guardians of eligible students will be notified via Parents Gateway
(PG) or email by <strong>Friday, 19 December 2025</strong>. Students who
opt to study Higher Malay Language and Higher Tamil Language will do so
at MOE-designated Higher Mother Tongue Language centres.</p>
<p>An online engagement session for further clarification on HMTL matters
will be held on <strong>Monday, 22 December</strong> at <strong>9 am</strong>.
The online meeting link will be shared with parents of eligible students
via Parents Gateway on <strong>Friday, 19 December</strong>.</p>
<p>Parents/Guardians are required to indicate their acceptance via PG, for
their child/ward to undertake HMTL, by <strong>Tuesday, 23 December 2025, 5 pm</strong>.
Please note that late submissions will not be accepted.</p>
<p><em>*Parents who are not onboarded to PG will be contacted via email</em>
</p>
