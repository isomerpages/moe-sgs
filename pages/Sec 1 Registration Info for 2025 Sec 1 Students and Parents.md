<h4><strong><u>Section E: Books and Uniform for approved Sec 1 FAS</u></strong></h4>
<p><strong>Sec 1 FAS approved by their Primary Schools</strong>
</p>
<p>Parent(s)/Guardians of Sec 1 students whose MOE Financial Assistance Scheme
(MOEFAS) applications have been approved by their Primary Schools are to
make an <strong>appointment</strong> to <strong>collect</strong> their <strong>books</strong> and <strong>uniform on-site</strong> in <strong>Serangoon Garden Secondary School</strong> via
the appointment link below.</p>
<p>On the day of your appointment, please go to the General Office to <strong>collect</strong> your <strong>MOEFAS approval letter</strong>  <strong><u>before</u></strong> making
your way to the bookshop or uniform shop for collection. Please <strong><u>do not</u></strong> purchase
the books and/or uniforms <strong><u>before</u></strong> collecting the MOEFAS
approval letter.</p>
<p>Appointment booking for 19 Dec - 30 Dec* - <a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/2024sgs18-30dec</a>
</p>
<p><em>* Appointment for 30 Dec is only for the school bookshop and not for the uniform shop</em>
</p>
<p><strong>New MOEFAS applicants / Applicants for School-based FAS</strong>
</p>
<p>If you wish to apply for Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS), please go
to&nbsp;<a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/moe-efas</a> to
submit your FAS application with the required supporting documents. Alternatively,
you may submit hardcopy documents to our General Office.</p>
<p>You will be informed of the application outcome once the application has
been processed.</p>
<h4><strong><u>Section F: Personalised Digital Learning Programme (PDLP)</u></strong></h4>
<p>The use of the Personal Learning Devices (PLD) for teaching and learning
aims to enhance digital literacy skills and foster self-directed and collaborative
learning in our students. It is essential for all students to bring their
PLD to school daily for their lessons.</p>
<p>The school will provide more details to parents in mid-Jan 2025, on the
process to purchase a PLD for their children. &nbsp;In the meanwhile, please
refer to the resource <a href="https://www.sgs.moe.edu.sg/pdlp/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> for more information about
the purchase of PLD.</p>

lessons are ongoing. We recommend that students prepare a healthy snack
such as a whole-meal sandwich, bun, fruits, nuts or a healthy snack bar.
Sweets, chocolates and any form of chips/crackers are discouraged. Regarding
drinks, only plain water may be consumed in class.</p>
