<p>As part of Racial Harmony Day celebrations, students participated in activities
like ethnic games, singing songs in different languages, and CCE lessons
on inclusivity and social cohesion. CCE Ambassadors set up booths featuring
traditional dishes and games, and conducted a live quiz during assembly.
Through these experiences, students deepened their appreciation of racial
harmony and celebrated the vibrant heritage of Singapore's multi-cultural
and multi-ethnic society.</p>
