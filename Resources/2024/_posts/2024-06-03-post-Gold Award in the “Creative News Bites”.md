<p>Congratulations to Yee Zhen, Josiah, and Elias for attaining the Gold
Award in the “Creative News Bites”《创新新闻60秒》category at the 2024 3rd Young
Reporter News Challenge《我来报新闻》!</p>
<p>Our students demonstrated creativity in addressing the theme of online
ticket fraud, by adapting Chinese lyrics to Taylor Swift’s “Shake It Off”.</p>
