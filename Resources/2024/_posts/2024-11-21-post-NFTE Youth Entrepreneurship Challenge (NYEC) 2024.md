<p>Nine months of hard work paid off at the annual NFTE Youth Entrepreneurship
Challenge (NYEC) 2024! Our students, Jayden Tan (3RS), Adly Rizky (3RS),
and Remus Pek (3EP), who were amongst the 46 finalists from Singapore,
achieved remarkable success, winning the Most Investible Idea Award and
the Ann Tan Inspire Excellence Award which recognises young individuals
with the potential to create positive societal change.</p>
<p>Our heartiest congratulations to Jayden, Adly, and Remus on their outstanding
achievements!</p>
<p>In addition, Ashley Goh (4EP) our Global Young Entrepreneur (Singapore)
2023 and National champion for NYEC 2023 is currently in New York City,
representing Singapore in the NYEC International Edition. Wishing Ashley
all the best in the competition!</p>
