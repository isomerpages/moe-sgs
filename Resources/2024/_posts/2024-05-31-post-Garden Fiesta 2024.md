<p>In an evening of colours, hopes and dreams, four friends brought the full-house
audience at the Ho Bee Auditorium (UCC), on their time-travelling journey
to save the Earth. Along the way, they met the elements of Air, Water and
Fire.</p>
<p>'Garden Fiesta V: Kaleidoscope of Sustainability' invited the audience
to reflect on the impact of our actions on the environment, and our future
generations; and for all to resolve to be a force for positive change.</p>
