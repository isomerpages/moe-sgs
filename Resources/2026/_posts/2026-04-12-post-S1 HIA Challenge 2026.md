<p>This year’s S1 Humanities-in-Action Challenge, co-organised by SGS and
AMKSS, brought together students from S1 cluster schools and beyond, for
an afternoon of real-world Humanities learning, where students applied
their knowledge of History, Geography, and Social Studies, critically and
inventively, whilst collaborating with peers from other schools.</p>
<p>As they reflected on how Shared Stories could inspire a Shared Future,
participants rose to the challenge by designing and presenting their AI-generated
blueprints for a 'We-First' Singapore.</p>
