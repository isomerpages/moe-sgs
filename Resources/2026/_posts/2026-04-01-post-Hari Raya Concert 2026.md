<p>For students, By students</p>
<p>Our student-led Hari Raya Concert 2026 brought the community together.
From a rousing Kompang entrance, to a Silat display, Malay dance, a fashion
parade, and quiz segments, students cheered for their friends who performed
with great passion and talent. It was a heartfelt celebration of Raya traditions
and teamwork, showing what students can achieve when they lead the way.</p>
