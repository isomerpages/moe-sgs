<p>It was a week filled with excitement as SGS hosted students and teachers
from Sekolah Tinggi Perempuan Raja Isteri (STPRI), Brunei, for the Inaugural
School Twinning Programme in May.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/stpri_brunei.jpg">
</div>
<p>Over five enriching days, our Brunei friends journeyed with SGS buddies
to experience school life in Singapore, including hands-on Applied Learning
Modules in Interior Design, Forensic Science, Early Childhood Education,
and Business &amp; Entrepreneurship. The week was filled with wonderful
cultural exchanges, from a traditional kompang welcome and a gulingtangan
performance by STPRI students, to a communal Nasi Ambeng meal and Teh Tarik-making
session at Kampong Glam, whilst exploring iconic Singapore landmarks. We
are grateful for the friendships forged and memories made and we look forward
to many more years of meaningful ties between SGS and STPRI!</p>
