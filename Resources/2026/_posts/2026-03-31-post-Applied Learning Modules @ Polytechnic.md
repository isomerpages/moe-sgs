<p>From science laboratories to creative studios, SGS students embarked on
an exciting three-day Applied Learning Modules @ Polytechnic experience!
From tackling real-world challenges such as water treatment, gene therapy,
and food innovation at Temasek Polytechnic, to exploring animation, visual
effects, and game development at Nanyang Polytechnic, students gained deeper
insights into post-secondary pathways and industries.</p>
