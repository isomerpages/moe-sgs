<p>Our curious Computing &amp; Robotics students attended the {build} Hackathon
finale at GovTech to see what happens when GovTechies turn everyday problems
into real solutions.</p>
<p>They walked through the showcase, talked to teams, and saw working prototypes
built over 6 weeks - from problem statement to pitch-ready product. What
stood out for them?</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Solutions that actually have a path to production</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cross-functional teams with designers, engineers, and product folks</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A focus on citizen pain points, not just cool tech</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>It was a behind-the-scenes look at how GovTech works to improve our quality
of life. We thank GovTech (special thanks to CSR Council and Hackathon
organisers) for this learning opportunity. Our students left with plenty
of ideas, and definitely some inspiration to join next time!</p>
