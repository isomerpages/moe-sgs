<p>SGS students’ personal voices and creativity were showcased as they garnered
3 Gold, 23 Silver, and 45 Bronze awards at the Queen's Commonwealth Essay
Writing Competition 2025 and 4 Gold and 4 Silver awards at the Inter-School
Malay Language Essay Writing Contest ‘Pena Emasku 2025’. Congratulations
to all SGS writers!</p>
