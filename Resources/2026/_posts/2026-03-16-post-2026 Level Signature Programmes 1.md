<p>Secondary One students embarked on an exciting journey of self-discovery
through a series of enriching workshops. Through learning a new language
in the Conversational Mother Tongue programme, trying out Tchoukball in
the Sports Exposure Programme, appreciating nature and learning about sustainability
through their LJs to various parks, and exploring hands-on experiments
in the Science Enrichment Workshop, students discovered new interests and
talents along the way. The LSP culminated in a Self-Leadership Workshop,
where students reflected on their strengths and developed the confidence
to take charge of their own growth.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Secondary 2 students embarked on enriching learning journeys to the National
Museum and Singapore Discovery Centre, exploring Singapore’s history and
culture. They also participated in a Financial Literacy Workshop, a Peer
Leadership Workshop, and a series of Applied Learning Modules, where they
gained hands-on experience in diverse areas such as pastry and dessert
making, interior design, business, healthcare, forensic science and automotive
engineering. Through it all, students explored their interests, applied
learning to real-world contexts, and forged new friendships.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Secondary 4 and 5 students participated in a range of meaningful experiences
as they explored post-secondary educational pathways. Students from 4 Gratitude
and 4 Wisdom engaged in Values-in-Action (VIA) projects at PCF Sparkle
Care Active Aging Centres, where they organised meaningful activities and
engaged the beneficiaries while learning to care for the needs of the community.
They also visited the Singapore Discovery Centre, gaining insights into
the importance of resilience as a nation, as we move forward into the future.</p>
