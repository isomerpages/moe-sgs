<p>Secondary 3 students from the Humanities Development Programme embarked
on a learning journey to Vidacity. Organised in partnership with the Urban
Redevelopment Authority (URA) and City Sprouts, the session deepened students’
understanding of Singapore’s urban planning process as they reflected on
sustainable land use, community-centric planning, and what it takes to
build a city not just for today, but for generations to come.</p>
