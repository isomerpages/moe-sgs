<p>GETB@SGS, develops our students to be confident and effective communicators.</p>
<p>Participants of this year’s S1 GETB competition included students from
all seven secondary schools in the South One Cluster. Presenting on topics
related to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDG) and
the celebration of SG60, participants shared their personal perspectives,
stories and experiences as active and concerned citizens of society.</p>
<p>Special thanks to our Guest of Honour, Mr Ling Khoon Chow, our esteemed
judges, Ms Komathi Kesavan, Mrs Elaine Yeo, and Dr Christine Xavier for
making the occasion extra special!</p>
<p>Congratulations to all award winners this year and we can’t wait to see
all of you next year!</p>
