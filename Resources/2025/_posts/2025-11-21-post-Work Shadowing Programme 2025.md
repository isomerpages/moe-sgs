<p>School’s out but learning doesn’t have to stop! Sec 3 students took part
in a Work Shadowing Programme (WSP) to experience the working world and
learn more about the various industries. Their journey of learning started
with self-discovery where they became more aware of their strengths, interests
and passions, and culminated in an attachment at the organisation of their
choice where they were guided by work mentors. It was an eye-opening and
enriching experience. A big Thank You to all our WSP partners for supporting
and journeying alongside our students in their learning!</p>
