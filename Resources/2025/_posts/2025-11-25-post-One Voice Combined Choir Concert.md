<p>The SGS Choir had the pleasure of hosting the One Voice Combined Choir
Concert, welcoming the student Choirs of Compassvale Secondary School,
North Vista Secondary School, and Kuo Chuan Presbyterian Secondary School.
The four Choirs came together as One, showcasing to family and friends,
not only musical talent, but also the spirit of collaboration and camaraderie
that the love of music fosters. Kudos to all students for their dedication
and passion!</p>
