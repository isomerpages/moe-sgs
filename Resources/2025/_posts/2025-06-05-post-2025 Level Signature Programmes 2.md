<p>During the second series of the Level Signature Programme (LSP2), Sec
1 students embarked on a journey of self-discovery. At Singapore Botanic
Gardens, they explored our rich heritage and discovered the beauty in our
UNESCO World Heritage site. Through Code for Fun, they unleashed their
digital talents, problem-solving skills, and creativity in the tech world
- who knows what future innovators we have among us! At Pasir Ris Park,
they became ‘detectives’, applying geographical knowledge from the classroom
to real-world coastal environments. As they discovered their passions,
enriched their skills, and celebrated their diverse talents, our students
grew in curiosity and adaptive thinking.</p>

<p>In the second series of the Level Signature Programme (LSP2), Sec 2 students
celebrated growth as individuals, and as a connected community. Through
cultural journeys, they discovered Singapore's rich tapestry of faiths
and heritage.Real-world exploration took them behind the scenes of creative
industries and innovative sustainability solutions, enriching their perspectives
on career possibilities and environmental responsibility. Leadership and
financial literacy workshops equipped them with essential life skills.
Applied Learning Modules provided exciting hands-on learning experiences:
making delightful pastry, video storytelling, sports leadership, healthcare
innovation, and F1 engineering challenges! Every workshop strengthened
friendships. Every journey deepened connections. Every challenge built
resilience together!</p>

<p>During the second series of the Level Signature Programme (LSP2), Sec
3 students embarked on farm visits which deepened their understanding of
how food security impacts our nation's future. ECG Careers Day opened doors
to diverse industries, enriching their understanding of real-world challenges
and future opportunities, whilst appreciating behind-the-scenes work that
keep Singapore running smoothly, from hospitality to transportation systems.
Public Speaking Skills workshops empowered them with confidence to lead
conversations and present ideas boldly. Interclass competitions provided
opportunities to exercise sportsmanship, teamwork, and peer support. Through
it all, they celebrated their growth as confident young leaders ready to
make active choices for positive impact!</p>
